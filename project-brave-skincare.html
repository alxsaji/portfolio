Project Overview

Developed a complete digital marketing strategy and brand system for Brave Skincare, a dermatologist-tested men's skincare line targeting urban Canadian professionals during the Postgraduate Digital Marketing program at York University.

The project involved creating a comprehensive brand identity, multi-channel content strategy, paid advertising campaigns, and marketing automation workflows to launch and position a new men's skincare brand in the competitive Canadian market.

The Challenge

While everyone is prone to skin issues, Canadian men aged 25-40 in major cities face severe winter skin challenges but lack skincare solutions that feel masculine, science-backed, and efficient. The market is saturated with either clinical brands that feel sterile or lifestyle brands lacking credibility, leaving a gap for a brand that speaks directly to men's needs.

Business Objectives

  • Launch a men's skincare brand targeting urban Canadian professionals (CA$70K+ income)
  • Drive profitable purchases during peak winter months (November-February)
  • Build brand awareness and credibility through science-backed positioning

Research & Discovery

Audience Research

Conducted competitive analysis and social media audits to identify two primary audiences:

  • Millennials (28-40): Busy professionals and parents valuing results, trust, and sustainability; prefer YouTube, email, and clinic partnerships
  • Gen Z (25-27): Career starters seeking efficiency and social proof; engage primarily on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Reddit

Persona Development

Created "Jordan," the primary persona: 32-year-old marketing manager in Toronto earning CA$85K, struggles with winter dryness and urban pollution, values transparency and efficiency.

Competitor Analysis

Analyzed men's skincare Instagram ads to identify winning patterns:

  • UGC and creator-style content builds trust and native engagement
  • Text-free visuals with real texture shots outperform overly polished product shots
  • Winter-specific messaging resonates in Canadian markets

Brand Strategy & Identity

Brand Positioning

Simple, science-backed skincare for Canadian men facing harsh winters. Positioned between clinical dermatology brands and lifestyle skincare with messaging focused on transparency, efficiency, and real results.

Visual Identity

Developed complete brand system including:

  • Brand colors, typography, and logo design
  • Style guide for consistent cross-channel execution
  • Masculine, winter-ready visual language using glass bottles and urban Canadian backdrops

Core Product

Three-step Winter Defense routine (cleanser, moisturizer, SPF) designed for simplicity and winter-specific protection.

Marketing Execution

Content Strategy

Created multi-channel content amplification strategy:

  • Blog/SEO: Published "How to survive your first Toronto winter without dry cracked skin: 3-minute routine for men" targeting keywords like "Toronto winter skin men" and "dry skin winter Canada"
  • Email Marketing: Designed drip-email campaign integrating brand assets and persona-driven messaging
  • Podcast Concept: Developed "Brave Radio" — science-backed skincare podcast featuring real stories and Canadian winter survival tips

Paid Advertising

Designed Instagram and Facebook ad campaigns aligned to conversion objectives:

  • Ad Concept 1: Real-world winter proof featuring man applying product during Toronto commute with visible texture shots and benefit callouts
  • Ad Concept 2: Quick routine demo (4:5 static format) with three-step layout against winter city backdrop matching brand aesthetic

Applied 2025 Instagram best practices: native UGC style, text-free visuals, winter-specific hooks, and clear CTAs.

Campaign Objectives & Metrics

  • Drive profitable purchases from men 25-40 in Toronto, Montreal, Vancouver, Calgary, Ottawa
  • Target revenue growth through Winter Defense routine adoption
  • Optimize for conversion, CTR, and remarketing quality

Reflection

This digital marketing strategy project for Brave Skincare provided valuable hands-on experience in brand development, multi-channel marketing, and data-driven campaign execution.

Integrated Brand Development & Marketing Strategy

I learned that successful digital marketing requires a cohesive brand system that translates across all channels. Developing the visual identity, messaging framework, and content strategy simultaneously ensured consistency and strengthened brand recognition. The brand system became the foundation for all marketing activities, from social media ads to email campaigns.

Audience Research Drives Strategy

Conducting thorough audience research and persona development was crucial for creating targeted campaigns. Understanding the differences between Millennial and Gen Z preferences, their platform usage, and content consumption habits allowed me to develop channel-specific strategies that resonated with each segment. The "Jordan" persona became a constant reference point for all creative and messaging decisions.

Multi-Channel Content Amplification

Creating a content amplification strategy that leveraged blog/SEO, email marketing, and podcast concepts demonstrated the importance of integrated marketing communications. Each channel served a specific purpose in the customer journey, from awareness (SEO content) to consideration (email nurture) to engagement (podcast). The key was ensuring all channels reinforced the same brand message and value proposition.

Data-Driven Campaign Optimization

Using Python for analytics and tracking performance across Google Search Console, Instagram Ads Manager, and HubSpot taught me the importance of continuous optimization. Setting SMART goals and KPIs upfront enabled data-driven decision making, allowing for iterative improvements based on real performance data rather than assumptions.
